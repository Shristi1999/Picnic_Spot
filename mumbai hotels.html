Mumbai Hotels

1)The Taj Mahal Palace, Mumbai:-

Hotel in South Mumbai, Mumbai (0.1 km from Gateway of India) Built in 1903, the iconic Taj Mahal Palace stands majestically opposite the Gateway of India,overlooking the Arabian Sea.

2)Hotel Suba Palace, Mumbai:-

Hotel in South Mumbai, Mumbai (0.3 km from Gateway of India) Situated near Gateway of India, Hotel Suba Palace offers free Wi-Fi and houses a multi-cuisine restaurant. 24-hour room service is available.

3)Fariyas Hotel,Mumbai:-

0.7 km from Gateway of India Located in Mumbai, Fariyas Hotel is a 5-minute drive from the Gateway of India. It features an outdoor swimming pool, 4 dining options and non-smoking rooms with free Wi-Fi.

4)Gulf Hotel,Mumbai:-

0.6 km from Gateway of India.Offering a restaurant that serves a selection of Indian delights, Gulf Hotel is located in Mumbai. Free WiFi access is available. Each room here will provide you with air conditioning.

5)Suncity Apollo Hotel,Mumbai:-

0.3 km from Gateway of India. Hotel Suncity Apollo is situated 1.8 km from Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CST) – a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It features a conference facility, a business centre, and complimentary Wi-Fi access.
