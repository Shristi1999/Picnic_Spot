Time To visit In Himachal Pradesh

Best Time to Visit Himachal Pradesh

Travel Season: Summer Monsoon Winter
For those who love nature and adventure, there's nothing quite like planning a trip to Himachal Pradesh. The hills beckon and with it, there's the promise of fresh, sweet air and splendid sights. Whether you're a mountain person or not, all it takes is a trip to the beautiful hills and mountains of Himachal Pradesh and you won't be immune to the allure of this wonderful state.

Summer In Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is often considered a summer holiday spot because even though the rest of the country faces sweltering heat during the months of May and June, the many towns and hill stations of Himachal Pradesh offer a respite. Many people consider summer to be the best time to visit Himachal, especially places such as McLeod Ganj and Dharamshala. Summer here lasts from March to June and the climate is pleasant with just a few thunderstorms and pre-monsoon showers cooling down the temperature further. During summer, there are plenty of domestic and international tourists who come to Himachal Pradesh to escape the heat of the cities. mer is also the best time to visit Himachal Pradesh if you're planning on doing some adventure sports such as trekking, camping, paragliding, kayaking, hot air ballooning and white-water rafting.

Monsoon In Himachal Pradesh

Monsoon in Himachal Pradesh is from July to September. The hills turn a lush green from all sides and Dalhousie gets most of the rain. However, although the rain turns the state mesmerising, there is an increased danger of landslides at this time so it's always better to be forewarned. While not so great for adventure seekers as trekking and hiking are not allowed. Nature' bounty is splendid during this season and with the light mist and beautiful atmosphere, you will have an unforgettable holiday if you visit Spiti, Kinnaur, Kasauli or McLeod Ganj. Most often, people avoid visiting Himachal Pradesh during the monsoons because of the unexpectedness of the rainfall and the possibility of landslides.

Winter In Himachal Pradesh

For those who are exceedingly fond of snow, winter is the best time to travel to Himachal Pradesh. Winter begins in October and lasts till February and December is the coldest month of the year. Places such as Manali, Narkanda, Kufri and Shimla enjoy good snowfall and visitors who have been waiting for snow can have an amazing time if they choose to visit Himachal Pradesh during winter. Since the temperature can be anywhere between 0 and 15 degrees, it's extremely important that you bring warm clothes and thermals when you travel to Himachal Pradesh in winter. Winter is great for several adventure lovers who can't wait to hit the mountains with their skis and there is a good deal of demand for flights to Himachal at this time. But at the same time, this is also off season and there are some good deals on accommodation. An important thing to remember is that there could be a number of hindrances caused by snow-packed roads.
