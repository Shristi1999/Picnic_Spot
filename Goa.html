GOA TOURISM

Goa, one of the smallest states of India, hides umpteen surprises in every corner. The land of sun and sea has some of the most pristine beaches in the country. If your destination of the holiday season is Goa then here is a list of famous beaches in Goa that you need to note down in your checklist.

Beaches Of Goa:-:-

1.Calangute Beach

Known as the ''Queen of Beaches'' in Goa, Calangute Beach caters to all types of travellers, from nature lovers to adventure junkies to party freaks. Acting as a haven for nature lovers, in particular, this beach is the biggest and the most popular one in North Goa, boasting of scintillating charm as well as beauty.

2.Hollant Beach

Which part of Goa:-South Goa.
Activities to do:-Diving, Kayaking,and Parasailing.
The Hollant beach is located at a distance of seven kilometers from the Bogmalo beach of the Vasco city. Thanks to low currents, the water here are shallow and it is one of the best Goa beaches to go swimming. One of the most isolated and most beautiful beaches in Goa, you will need a personal vehicle to reach this place.It is said that the Hollant beach is the only beach in Goa from where a clear sunset can be witnessed.

3.Velsao Beach

Which part of Goa:-South goa
Activities to do:-Diving,Shopping,and Parasailing
The Velsao beach, located very close to the Dabolim airport, boasts of unparalleled scenic beauty, is one of the most popular beaches in Goa. It is famous for the white shells and starfish that you can find all over the seashore. One of the most isolated beaches in Goa, the Velsao beach is perfect if you are looking for some peace, quiet, and solitude.

4.Arambol Beach

Which part of Goa:-North goa
Activities to do:-Surfing, Shopping, and Food
The Arambol beach is a famous beach in Goa and is a sixteen kilometer long stretch of shoreline, surrounded by cliffs on two sides. The beach has a freshwater lake and a chirpy market nearby. The relaxed vibe of Arambol makes it one of the most famous beaches in Goa. Arambol also hosts nigh parties and is popular as the capital of hippie culture in Goa.

5.Ashwem Beach

Which part of Goa:-North Goa
Activities to do:-Viewpoints, Hanging out, Parasailing
The famous Arambol beach, Ashwem is another name from the list of most isolated beaches in Goa. At any time of the year, even during the tourist season, there are hardly any people around. The Ashwem beach is famous because this is where the Olive Ridley Turtles come ashore to lay their eggs.

6.Palolem Beach

Which part of Goa:-South goa
Activities to do:-Shopping, Food, Partying, and Diving
The Palolem beach lies in the extreme South Goa and sits at a distance of twenty-five kilometers from the Margao city. Palolem has an array of delicious restaurants and cafes making it one of the most popular beaches in Goa. Tourists can also indulge in watersports like paragliding and wind-surfing at this beach. Hiking around this beach is one of the most popular things to do in Goa.

7.Baga Beach

Which part of Goa:-North Goa
Activities to do:-Motor boat rides, Jet skiing, Dolphin rides
The Baga beach, located at a distance of just two kilometers from Calangute beach. It looks like it has come straight out of a painting. Baga Beach is one of the most popular places to visit in Goa. It's because of the luxury resorts and beach cottages. The beach gets its name from the Baga Creek, which meets the Arabian Sea at the northern end of the beach. It's one of the most magnificent beaches of Goa.

8.Miramar Beach

Which part of Goa:-North Goa
Activities to do:-Water sports, Bar hopping and Food
Miramar beach is one of the popular beaches in Goa, located just three kilometers from Panjim, the capital city of Goa. The Miramar beach boasts of a soft, silvery sand bed, and a vast expanse of fine golden sand flecked with palm groves. As one of the famous beaches in Goa, Miramar offers pristine views of Mandovi River meeting the Arabian Sea. You can also go for the river cruise.

9. Vagator Beach

Which part of Goa:-North Goa
Activities to do:-Partying, Shopping, and Water Sports
Located at a distance of nine kilometers from the Mapusa city,this famous beach in goa resembles a crescent moon. The monotony of fine white sand is broken by beautifully contrasting black rocks. The lush greenery around this beach will definitely take your breath away.

10.Bogmalo Beach

Which part of Goa:-South Goa
Activities to do:-Bar Hopping, Water Sports, and Food
The Bogmalo Beach, is at a distance of five kilometers from the Dabolim airport. It was originally a small fishing hamlet which is now expanding into a tourist hub. The nearness to the airport has made Bogmalo one of the most famous beaches in Goa frequented by tourists and backpackers.

11.Majorda Beach

Which part of Goa:-South Goa
Activities to do:-Shopping, Surfing, Snorkeling
Among all the beaches in Goa, there is none like Majorda. The scenic beauty is bound to make you awestruck. This beach destination is full of canopy of palm trees, clear blue water, and soft white sand. Tourists love this beach for its peaceful atmosphere and serenity.
