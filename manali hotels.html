Manali Hotels

1)Apple Country Resorts:-

1.6 km from centre Located in Manali, Apple Country Resorts- A Vegetarian Place has a spa and wellness centre - Tatva. The property operates a 24-hour front desk. Free Wi-Fi and free parking are provided

2)Hotel Mountain :-

Set in Manali, 5 km from Devi Temple, Hotel Mountain face by Snow City Hotels offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a shared lounge and a garden.

3)Snow Valley Resorts:-

1.7 km from centre Tall pine trees and fragrant apple orchards surround Snow Valley Resorts, an eco-friendly resort in the pollution-free zone of Manali's Log Hut Area. Free Wi-Fi is available in all areas.

4)Larisa Resort Manali:-

9 km from centre Offering an outdoor pool and a restaurant, Larisa Resort is located 15.4 km from Manali. Free WiFi access is available in this resort. The resort offers spa facilities and sauna.
