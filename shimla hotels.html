Shimla Hotels

1)Snow Valley Resorts:-

Featuring free WiFi, Snow Valley Resorts offers accommodation 5.7 km from The Mall Road. Free private parking is available on site. Professional Staff, Beautiful Location

2)Wildflower Hall:-

Featuring luxurious rooms with views of a garden or the surrounding mountains, Wildflower Hall Shimla boasts heated indoor and outdoor pools. One of the best hotels i have visited in India. Fireplaces in the public places are awesome.

3)Radisson Hotel Shimla:-

Sitting at the foot of the north-west Himalayas, Radisson Hotel enjoys beautiful views of the surrounding cedar forests and mountains. Everything else,room stay was very comfortable, the services were spot on restaurant was great,spread was relatively good front office/housekeeping is very good.

4)Clarkes hotel:-

Located on Mall Road, the main street of Shimla and 600 metres from The Ridge and Scandal Point, Clarkes Hotel operates a 24-hour front desk to assist guests.

5)Welcomhotel By ITC Hotels:-

Situated in Shimla, 12 km from The Mall Road,Welcomhotel By ITC Hotels Shimla, features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a fitness centre and a bar. Serene, awesome view and quite place
