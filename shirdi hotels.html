Shirdi Hotels

1)Hotel Laxmibai Palace:-

Offering a 24-hour front desk, Hotel Sai Pushpak is located just 100 metres from the Sai Baba Temple and 400 metres from the Sainagar Bus Station. Free WiFi.

2)Hotel Yogiraj:-

Located in Shirdi, 400 metres from Shri Adinath Shewtamber Jain Mandir, Hotel Yogiraj provides accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking and a shared lounge. Location and staff was very friendly.

3)Sun-n-Sand Shirdi:-

Located in the pilgrim city of Shirdi, the 5-star Sun N Sand hotel features an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness club, as well as a spa and wellness centre. Complimentary private parking is provided. I like my entire stay including shuttle services pool breakfast and the way staff treated .

4)Hotel G-Square - Shirdi:-

Hotel G-Square (Shirdi) is located just opposite the holy temple of Sai Baba. The Shirdi Bus Station is 100 metres. Free WiFi access is available in the public areas of the property.

5)Hotel Disha Palace:-

Barely 100 metres from the holy shrine of Saibaba, Hotel Disha Palace operates a 24-hour front desk to assist guests and offers free Wi-Fi in public areas. Free parking facility is available. Clean Room, courteous staff, great location.
